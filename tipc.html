    Tip Calculator is the ultimate solution for effortlessly calculating tips, ensuring that you always leave the right gratuity for your servers, whether you're dining at your favorite restaurant or grabbing takeout. With its user-friendly interface and versatile features, Tip Calculator takes the guesswork out of tipping and simplifies your dining experience.

    Key Features

    To work out the love score between two people:

    1. -> Accept the bill.
    2. -> Accepts the tip amount and calculates the tip for the percentage cost of services.
    3. -> Calculates the total bill
    4. -> Reset the fields.
